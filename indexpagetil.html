C O N T A C T

Here's a brief overview of my hobbies, and different interests :

Literature

The books I read play an important part
in my personality. Indeed, I read books of many kinds on a daily basis.
I am also casually writing, novels and poems.
Please check the "Books & culture" section to learn more...

Surfing

... surfing is the sport I am passionate about,
I had the chance to surf in several places around the world.
For me, this sport built a special connection with the ocean,
and by extension with all things natural...

Travelling

Thanks to my family and my studies,
I had the opportunity to travel to a myriad of different places.
Travelling, just as reading, opens the mind and forges the personality.
And, just as surfing, discovering marvelous natural places built for me a sensitivity
to the wilderness, going outdoors, and enjoying the delicate beauty of nature.

Cinema

I took interests in movies during my early adolescence,
I saw hundreds of movies, enjoyed several of them. A handful of them
also played an important role in defining who I am.
While studying in San Diego, I had the opportunity to follow
a Film & TV Production class, during which I learned
the basics of directing, framing, operating cameras, writing scripts,
and I had the chance to direct a scene for a TV show.

Rowing

Willing to find a watersport that can be practiced more regularly than surfing, as swell can be inconsistent, I recently picked up rowing.
Gliding with speed on the crystal-clear sea surface, thanks to the strength and the technical skills of coordinated fellow rowers, makes it a fascinating sport. It is forging discipline and self-discipline, as well as allowing a moment of escape, only focusing on the cadence regularity and the coordination with others.

Skiing

I picked up skiing early. Only being three years old,
my parents threw me on the snow-covered slopes.
Since then, I never quit, and have always been
looking forward to hearing about the first snowfall of the winter.
Just like surfing, skiing is a peculiar sport as it is using
as support a unique natural feature,
and to me,
the two most thrilling of those are the mountains and the waves.
